## Core Purpose
The EmpowerFiX `Marketplace` is an interface where users can do private exchanges and/or buying and selling of goods or services with other members of the Empower Humanity 2.0 trusted network. This is for individuals not vendors accepting Stellar asset as payment. Think of it as eBay for people who want to buy and sell using Stellar assets. It can be physical or tokenized items or services. Tokenized items or services MUST be redeemable for ACTUAL items or services. Having a private Marketplace where a trusted network can list or search for products, services, or tokenized assets by category and negotiate exchange conditions and transcact in Stellar assets is a stepping stone to co-creating a sovereign economy with news ways to exchange value using the "Stellar Energy Asset System" (SEAS).

## Components
- **Sell Item/Service/Tokenized Asset** - User can list an item, service, or tokenized asset they want to sell
- **Buy Item/Service/Tokenized Asset** - User can search for or list an item, service, or tokenized asset they want to purchase
- **Asset Escrow** - Exchange or transfer of Stellar assets is held until items or services have been received
- **Buyer/Seller Ratings (Resonance)** - Users can rate buyers and sellers for integrity in transactions; in tiers 2+ a resonance indicator replaces ratings

### Sell Item/Service/Tokenized Asset
User can list an item, service, or tokenized asset for sale at an asking price in USDC (for universal familiarity of value), but can also list in which assets they prefer or are willing to be paid. An auto-calculator determines the estimated non-USDC asset amount equivalent. Potential buyers can either `buy instantly` or `make an offer` (in USDC). The seller can either decline or accept an offer.

### Buy Item/Service/Tokenized Asset
User can search for or list an item, service, or tokenized asset they want to buy. When listing something they want to buy in the `"Looking to Buy"` section the user must give a price (in USDC) that they are willing to pay for the item, service, or tokenized asset. If a seller wants to entertain a buy offer they can either `accept offer` or `make counter offer` which will create a listing and send a link to the item in the "Sell Item/ Service/Tokenized Asset" screen. The buyer can either decline or accept the counter offer and can also list in which assets they prefer or are willing to be paid. An auto-calculator determines the estimated non-USDC asset amount equivalent.

An example agreement might look like this:

A person wants to sell a used laptop and posts the item with a video showing the used laptop functioning and cleared of old data and the price in USDC. Another person likes what they see and agrees to buy it for the listed price. The buyer and seller write any terms they wish to be included in the agreement, and EmpowerBot drafts a smart contract that can be viewed and includes layman's explanations of the code. The buyer sends the USDC to the Asset Escrow smart contract address that will hold the funds until the buyer receives the item and feels the agreement was satisfied. The buyer then clicks a button to release the funds from escrow saying the item was received and gives a rating to the seller.When the seller receives the funds they are prompted to rate the buyer as well. If the buyer fails to release the funds after an agreed upon period of time after receiving the item, the funds will be automatically released from escrow. Any disputes beyond that are left to the parties to work out. EmpowerBot can mediate grievances, but both parties must agree before entering a private contract that this cannot be brought into a public jurisdiction.
